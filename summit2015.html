InnerSource Summit

November 9, 2015
San Jose, CA USA

Companies today already understand that Open Source is necessary.

Less is understood about how to effectively use Open Source principles on internal development. This is the challenge that InnerSource seeks to addresses.

The first InnerSource Summit will be in San Jose on Monday, November 9. We will spend the day discussing our current challenges with bringing InnerSource to more traditional organizations, sharing what we all have learned, and setting up for success over the next several years.

AGENDA

  • Sunday November 8
    • Dinner and networking
  • Monday November 9
    • Opening remarks
    • Case studies
    • Lunch
    • Leadership remarks
    • Community of Change workshop
    • Closing and reception

Are you working to implement InnerSource in your organization?

Each of our journeys is unique. Please consider sharing a short (20-30 minute) case study at the Summit. We would love to hear about your experience bringing InnerSource practices to your organization, particularly what your current objectives are, what experiments you are running, what you have learned so far (from successful or failed experiments), what culture changes you are trying to create, and challenges you haven't yet solved.

If you are interested in joining the conversation at the Summit and can join us on November 9, please Email Us. We will do our best to accommodate everyone while we also try to keep the event small enough to enable every voice to be heard

Looking forward to seeing you there!
